## 🎯 Learning Tips

### 1. Go Slow
Don't rush! It's better to really understand one chapter than to skim through all of them.

### 2. Type Everything
Don't copy/paste code. Typing helps your fingers learn and helps you catch mistakes.

### 3. Do All Exercises
The exercises are where real learning happens. Don't skip them!

### 4. Break Things
Try changing the examples. Make mistakes on purpose. See what error messages say.

### 5. Take Breaks
Your brain needs time to process. Take a break between chapters.

### 6. Ask Questions
Stuck? That's normal! Re-read slowly, check the solution, experiment.

### 7. Build Your Own
After each chapter, try making your own program that uses what you learned.
